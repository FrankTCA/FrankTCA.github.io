ColorChat Mod

ColorChat Requires no Minecraft Forge to run!

Have you ever wanted to make your Minecraft colorful?

Then learned about this great symbol (Yes, the § symbol)

Then realized that you can't actually enter that symbol into the chat

Sad story, isn't it?

Well now you can enter that symbol with the simple installation of a non-forge mod!

You need no Minecraft Forge to run this mod, making it easy to put in with your normal client!

How to install:

First, download the Mod from the download link below!

Next, unzip the zip file.

Then, follow the simple instructions on README.txt
